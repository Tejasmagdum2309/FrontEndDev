Our commitment to security

SOC 2 & Penetration Testing

Arpari has achieved SOC 2 certification. We additionally work with third-party cybersecurity experts to conduct regular penetration testing to detect vulnerabilities.

Trusted Partners

When we work with third parties to help provide services to you, we select leading partners who have adopted equally stringent security protocols.

Tokenization

Arpari uses tokenization to protect sensitive information by replacing it with a secure surrogate ID, called a token. That way, we only store that token, not your actual data, ensuring your information is always safe.

End-to-End Encryption

All data is transmitted with encryption-in-transit using HTTPS or similar protocols. All data is securely stored with encryption-at-rest using AES-256 or higher standards.
